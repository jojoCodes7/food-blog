what is ofada rice

Ofada rice and stew is a traditional Nigerian dish that originates from the South West region of the country. This flavorful and aromatic meal is known for its unique combination of rice and stew made with locally sourced ingredients, giving it a distinct taste that is loved by many.

Brief history about ofada rice

Ofada rice and stew is a traditional Nigerian dish that originates from the South West region of the country. This flavorful and aromatic meal is known for its unique combination of rice and stew made with locally sourced ingredients, giving it a distinct taste that is loved by many.

Ingredients in making ofada rice

  • Ofada rice (2 cups)
  • Water (4 cups)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Banana or plantain leaves (for wrapping)
