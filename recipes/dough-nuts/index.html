what are doughnuts?

A doughnut or donut is a type of food made from leavened fried dough. It is popular in many countries and is prepared in various forms as a sweet snack that can be homemade or purchased in bakeries, supermarkets, food stalls, and franchised specialty vendors.

Ingredients on how to make doughnuts

  1. 1 ¼ cups Milk
  2. 2 ¼ teaspoons Active Dry Yeast
  3. 2 Eggs
  4. 8 tablespoons Butter (melted and cooled)
  5. ¼ cup Granulated Sugar
  6. 1 teaspoon Salt
  7. 4 ¼ cups All-Purpose Flour (plus more for rolling out the dough)
  8. 2 quarts Neutral Oil (for frying, plus more for the bowl)
