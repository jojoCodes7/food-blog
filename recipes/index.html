How to make different nigerian recipes

we will first talk about ofada rice

ingredients

  1. 2cups ofada rice
  2. water
  3. salt

procedures on how to make ofada rice

  • Start with the sorting. Pour the rice on a tray and pick out the tiny stones and other dirty particles present in the rice.
  • After sorting, pour rice into a bowl then wash repeatedly to make sure it’s clean from dirt and the water becomes clean.
  • Then pour 3 cups of water into a pot and put on fire. Add the washed rice and parboil for about 10 minutes.
  • Now turn off the heat and rinse out the starch this also helps the rice to remain in a single form instead of being soggy.
  • Pour the parboiled rice back into the pot. Then add 2cups of water and salt. Cover the pot and allow to cook.
  • Remove from the heat once cooked.
  • Drain out any excess water using a colander or sieve. If you don’t like draining you can as well allow the water to dry up before turning off the heat.

ingredients used for making ofade rice stew

  2. 3 large red bell peppers
  3. 2tbsp Iru(fermented locust beans)
  4. ½cup palm oil
  5. 2 medium onions
  6. 2tbsp crayfish (optional)
  7. Ponmo (chopped)
  8. Hard-boiled eggs (optional)
  9. Assorted meat of your choice(Chopped)
  10. 3 large tomatoes (optional)
  11. 1 smoked fish
  12. Seasoning cubes
  13. salt to taste
